ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Chandra Lawson and I have been writing résumés and CVs for more than ten years. In the private and public sectors, I worked as a medical secretary and medical administrative assistant. My professional training includes JIST-Farr employment and job search seminars and résumé writing/grammar workshops, as well as, learning from online forums with human resource managers and colleagues. My work is published in "Expert Résumés for Healthcare Careers", by JIST Works, ©2004.

Currently, I provide service exclusively to those in the healthcare field; specifically, Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation.
